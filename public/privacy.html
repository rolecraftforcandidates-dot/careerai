Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

RoleCraft ("we", "our", or "us") is operated by RoleCraft (India). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you use our platform at www.rolekraft.com.

By using RoleCraft, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following information when you register or use RoleCraft:

  • Name and email address — provided via our onboarding form or Google Sign-In
  • Resume / CV — uploaded voluntarily for ATS analysis and interview prep personalisation
  • Professional details — target role, experience level, tech stack (from onboarding form)
  • Usage data — tasks completed, questions answered, scores received
  • Payment information — processed securely by Razorpay; we do not store card numbers or bank details

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To generate your personalised 30-day interview preparation plan
  • To analyse your resume and provide ATS scores and improvement tips
  • To match you with relevant job listings
  • To send your login credentials and plan updates via email
  • To process payments and manage your subscription
  • To improve our platform and services

3. Data Storage

Your data is stored in Google Sheets (secured with Google Service Account credentials) and on Railway cloud infrastructure. Resume files are stored on Google Drive with restricted access.

We retain your data as long as your account is active. You may request deletion at any time by emailing us.

4. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services to operate RoleCraft:

  • Anthropic (Claude AI) — for generating personalised interview prep content
  • Google (Sheets, Drive, OAuth) — for data storage and login
  • Razorpay — for payment processing
  • Brevo (Sendinblue) — for transactional emails
  • Adzuna — for job listing data

Each of these services has their own privacy policy and data handling practices.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We only share data with the third-party services listed above, strictly as needed to operate the platform.

6. Cookies

We use session cookies to keep you logged in. These are essential for the platform to function and do not track you across other websites. No advertising or analytics cookies are used.

7. Your Rights

  • Access — You can request a copy of your data at any time
  • Correction — You can update your information via the Settings panel in your dashboard
  • Deletion — You can request complete account deletion by emailing us
  • Portability — You can request your data in a portable format

8. Security

We use industry-standard security practices including encrypted connections (HTTPS), hashed passwords (bcrypt), and restricted access to data stores. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

9. Children's Privacy

RoleCraft is intended for professionals aged 18 and above. We do not knowingly collect data from anyone under 18.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes via email or a notice on the platform.
